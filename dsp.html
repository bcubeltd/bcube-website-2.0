Digital Signal Processing

We are working in the area of DSP

A Breif Introduction

With our extensive digital signal processing skill set, we have the ability to approach even the most complex real-world problems head-on. Our experts have several publications in prestigious signal processing journals and conferences. Our digital signal processing group comprises PhD scholars from top US and Pakistani universities. Signal processing is essential for a wide range of applications. From commercial communication to military radar systems, signal processing helps utilize available resources to the fullest. We contributed to the development of Pakistan’s first fully indigenously produced radar, funded by the Ministry of Science and Technology (MoST) and the National University of Sciences & Technology (NUST). The GSR is a ground-based portable surveillance radar, and our experts developed its core signal processing algorithms, which have been proven effective in the field. The Pakistan Army is set to induct these radars into its arsenal. Additionally, our high-profile experts have developed a highly complex processor for a wide-band IF system, capable of real-time spectrum estimation, signal detection, and parameter estimation on incoming wide-band multi-gigahertz IF signals. The processor measures time and frequency domain parameters of incoming signals at nanosecond intervals.
