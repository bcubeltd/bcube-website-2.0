Design Engineer (RF/Microwave)

Key Responsibilities:

  • Development and validation of RF/Microwave circuits such as amplifiers, filters, multiplexers
  • Efficient integration of RF circuitry into mechanical enclosures
  • Design and evaluation of product prototypes and engineering samples
  • Development of test methods and systems
  • Documentation of test results, writing reports
  • Supporting the transition from prototype designs into production

Requirements:

  • BE/MS in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with emphasis on RF/Microwave circuit design
  • 2 to 3 years of experience in the field of RF/Microwave product development
  • An organized, detail-oriented, and methodical work approach
  • Knowledge of Microwave circuits and devices
  • Good verbal and written communication skills in English
  • Ability to work as a team member in a fast-paced professional environment
