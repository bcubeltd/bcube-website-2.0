Dual-channel 3U VPX Baseband Processor

BCube's 3U VPX Baseband Processor is a ruggedized embedded SDR transceiver designed for highly stable and reliable operation in harsh environments. The module features two full-duplex RF channels that can run on any frequency from 75MHz to 6.0GHz. It is 3U VPX compatible, supports industrial temperature range, durable and is perfectly suited for applications that require high frequency stability of a GPS synchronized clock.

Typical applications include

  • Radio signal detection and measurement
  • Radio protocol analysis for 2G, 3G, LTE and other wireless networks
  • Satellite and backhaul communications
  • Test bench and education equipment
