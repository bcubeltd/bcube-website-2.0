RF/Microwave Circuits & Systems

BCUBE is committed to providing RF and Microwave systems with high quality, advanced performance, and cost-effective components that enhance our customer’s ability to thrive in the most competitive and demanding business environments.

RF/Microwave Systems

BCUBE engineers have design, development, testing, and manufacturing expertise in RF/MW products for both defense and commercial applications from L to Ka Band. We have extensive experience in our product line of RF/MW systems.

We provide expert solutions for RF/MW systems for the commercial and defense industries. Our expertise and experience in the domain enable us to design and manufacture custom systems as per client needs. Some of the systems our experts have developed include:

  • AM, FM transmission system
  • FSK, QPSK, OFDM supported radio links
  • Audio & Video link
  • Air intelligence receiver
  • Broadband Microwave Receiver for EW Applications
  • Broadband RF signal generators
  • Conceptual Design
  • EMI/EMC Qualification
  • Service and maintenance

RF Modules

RF Application designs, develops, manufactures, and markets PLL synthesizers, Amplifiers, Filters, and Tools for RF microwave applications up to 20 GHz. Our products are primarily focused on wireless communications infrastructure, defense, aerospace, and satellite communications markets.

At BCUBE, we offer design services not only for systems but also for sub-systems. Our team of engineers has expertise in designing various RF/MW modules ranging from 100MHz to 20GHz. Some of the RF modules we have successfully designed include:

  • Direct Digital Synthesizer (DDS) Modules
  • PLL synthesizer
  • Low noise Amplifier design
  • High Power RF power amplifier design
  • RF frequency modulator
  • RF transmitter & Receiver
  • ASK / FSK transmitter & Receiver
  • RF up/down converter
  • Wireless solution
  • Commercial product development
  • LPF, HPF, BPF filter
  • Multiplexers
