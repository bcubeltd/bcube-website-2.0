Senior Mechanical Engineer

Position Overview:

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Mechanical Engineer with a strong background in Electromechanical systems. The ideal candidate will have over 5 years of experience in product design, integrating both electrical and mechanical components, and will be proficient in advanced simulation tools for CFD, structural, and thermal analysis.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead product design and development of mechanical systems, including integration with electrical components.
  • Perform advanced simulations (CFD, structural, thermal) to validate and optimize system performance.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams (electrical, manufacturing, quality) to ensure seamless product integration.
  • Prepare detailed engineering documentation, including CAD models, drawings, and technical reports.
  • Drive design reviews, risk assessments, and continuous improvement initiatives.
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical knowledge sharing within the team.

Qualifications:

  • Education: BE/MS in Mechanical or Mechatronics Engineering.
  • Experience: Minimum 5+ years in mechanical product design and development.

Technical Expertise:

  • Strong proficiency in CAD tools (SolidWorks, CATIA, or equivalent).
  • Hands-on experience with CFD, FEA, and thermal analysis software (ANSYS, COMSOL, or similar).
  • Knowledge of materials, manufacturing processes, and design for reliability.

Soft Skills:

  • Strong problem-solving ability, leadership, and effective communication skills.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience in multidisciplinary product development (electro-mechanical systems).
  • Familiarity with industry standards and compliance requirements.
  • Project management exposure with ability to handle multiple priorities.

How to Apply:

Send your CVs to jobs@bcubelimited.com. Don’t forget to add M-DE-22 reference to the email subject.
